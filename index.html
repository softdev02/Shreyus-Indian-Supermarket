~ About us ~

Shreyus Indian Supermarket

Shreyus Indian Supermarket is an Indian grocery store located in a quiet, but a convenient place in the Heart of Folsom,CA.

Shreyus brings its customers a wide variety of imported Indian goods at an affordable price, making them feel like they are in India, even though they are away from India. We tirelessly work to make sure that all major brands are available, even during this pandemic when food supply is drastically affected.
